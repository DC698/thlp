Receipt Acknowledgment

Authorized Use

I understand and agree that this fuel card is restricted exclusively to the purchase of motor fuel (gasoline or diesel) at Valero-branded fuel stations. The card's restricted terms and applicable DOL guidelines strictly limit use to fuel purchases only.

🚫 The following purchases are STRICTLY PROHIBITED
  • Alcohol or alcoholic beverages of any kind
  • Tobacco products, including cigarettes, cigars, and vaping products
  • Convenience store items (food, beverages, lottery tickets, or any non-fuel merchandise)
  • Cash advances or any transaction other than motor fuel

  • I will immediately report any lost, stolen, or compromised card to my Case Manager.
